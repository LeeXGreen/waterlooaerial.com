Are buyers seeing your properties from the best angle? A 2017 report based on MLS statistics concluded that homes with aerial photos sell up to 68% faster. Aerial photos give a great sense of the overall scope of a property, and can help to show the location relative to neighborhood landmarks.

According to a 2019 NAR report, 29 percent of realtors hire a professional to operate a drone for their business. This is up from 11 percent in 2017, and 23 percent in 2018.

Adding aerial shots to your walkthrough videos can help to set your listings apart from the crowd. Combining interior gimbal footage with smooth exterior drone footage makes for a professional result.
